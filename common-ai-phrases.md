9. **Agreement Phrases:**
   - "I agree that..."
   - "It is true that..."

   - "I support the idea that..."
   - "It is clear that..."

10. **Disagreement Phrases:**

These phrases help create coherent and structured content, making it easier for the AI to generate text that flows logically and persuasively.
